Customer Loyalty, Segmentation and Churn Analysis using RFM

Tools Used: Azure DataFactory, Azure Databricks, PySpark, Tableau

Customer Loyalty and Segmentation Analysis uses RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) modeling to understand customer behavior. It helps businesses divide customers into groups based on how recently, how often, and how much they buy. High-value customers drive significant revenue through large or frequent purchases.

Key Insights:
  • Customer Retention: Retaining loyal customers is more cost-effective and drives long-term business growth.
  • Spending Habits: High-value customers can be incentivized to purchase more frequently, boosting revenue.
  • Risk of Churn: At-risk customers need immediate re-engagement to prevent revenue loss.
  • Personalization Opportunity: Tailor approaches to different customer groups—reward loyal customers and re-engage at-risk customers with targeted campaigns.
Recommendations:
  • For Loyal Customers: Implement loyalty programs, exclusive offers, or VIP treatment to maintain engagement and increase customer lifetime value.
  • For High-Value Customers: Offer personalized discounts or early access to new products to encourage more frequent purchases.
